Wolfowitz connects reform with security U.S. Deputy Defense Secretary Paul Wolfowitz urged Japan on Monday to resolve its deflation and bad-loan problems, saying its economic recovery is vital for East Asian and world security.
"Let me be blunt," Wolfowitz told 60 Japanese and U.S. business leaders at the Japan-U.S. Business Conference. "Economic recovery in Japan is every bit as important to the security of Japan, the security of the United States and the security of the region as are the contributions of the Self-Defense Forces.
