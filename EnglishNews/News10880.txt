Sato blazes to victory Nobuhito Sato turned a one-stroke deficit into an overwhelming six-shot victory Sunday, shooting a final-round 64 to win the Japan Golf Tour Championship Iiyama Cup for his second major title on the Japanese circuit.
The 2000 Japan PGA Championship winner earned the 24 million yen winner's check out of the 120 million yen purse, which moved him to the top of the money list with total earnings of 71.6 million yen.
