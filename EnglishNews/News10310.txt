Clarke tied for Taiheiyo lead Darren Clarke battled jet lag to shoot a 6-under-par 66 and take a share of the first-round lead with Ryoken Kawagishi at the Mitsui-Sumitomo Visa Taiheiyo Masters on Thursday.
The 36-year-old from Northern Ireland made a solid start from the 10th at Taiheiyo Club, reeling off three birdies before picking up two more shots to make the turn at 31.
