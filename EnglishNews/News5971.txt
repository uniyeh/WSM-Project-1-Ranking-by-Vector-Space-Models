In 'unsafe' encounter, Chinese warship sails within 40 meters of U.S. Navy destroyer in South China Sea A Chinese destroyer performed an "unsafe" maneuver during an encounter with a U.S. Navy warship in the disputed South China Sea over the weekend, coming within 40 meters (45 yards) of the American vessel's bow and forcing it to steer the ship away to prevent a collision, the U.S. military said Tuesday.
