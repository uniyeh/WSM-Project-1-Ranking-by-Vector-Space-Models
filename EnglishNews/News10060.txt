Miyazato closing in on Otsuka title Ai Miyazato zeroed in on her fifth title of the season after extending her overnight lead to four strokes with a 4-under-par 68 in the second round of the Hisako Higuchi IDC Otsuka Ladies on Saturday.
Taiwan's Julie Lu and Kaori Higo both shot 68s to share second place but Keiko Sasaki had a 72 and dropped from second into a three-way tie for fourth with Misato Nishikawa and South Korea's Lee Jeong Eun.
