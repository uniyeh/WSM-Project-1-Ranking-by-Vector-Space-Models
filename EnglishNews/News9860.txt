Yeh one up at Sega Sammy Cup Overnight joint leader Yeh Wei-tze of Taiwan moved in front by one shot after shooting an even-par 72 in the third round of the Sega Sammy Cup on Saturday.
Yeh, who made three birdies and as many bogeys at The North Country Golf Club in Hokkaido, took sole possession of the lead with a birdie on the par-5 final hole that moved him to 6-under 210.
