Fudo looking strong at Golf 5 Yuri Fudo made a solid start in her defense of the Golf 5 Ladies title by shooting a 5-under-par 67 to take a one-shot lead after the first round on Friday.
Fudo, who produced a sensational comeback to win this event last year, made six birdies and a bogey at Alpen Golf Club's Bibai Course to establish a one-shot lead over Taiwan's Wei Yun-jye.
