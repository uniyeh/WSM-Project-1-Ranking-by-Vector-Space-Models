Chinese research ship detected in Japan's economic zone A Japan Coast Guard patrol vessel detected a Chinese research ship Sunday morning within Japan's exclusive economic zone, officials said.
The 3,235-ton Dongfangfong No. 2 was seen operating some 30 km west-northwest of Uotsuri, the main island of the Senkaku group in the East China Sea at around 9:30 a.m., the officials said.
