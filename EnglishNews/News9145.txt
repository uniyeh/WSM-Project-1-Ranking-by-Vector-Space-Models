Yokomine in position to win consecutive titles Sakura Yokomine put herself in contention for back-to-back titles with a 4-under 68 in the second round of the Life Card Ladies on Saturday.
The popular 23-year-old moved from a tie for seventh into a share of second with world No. 3 Shin Ji Yai at 3-under 141, one stroke behind Lee Ji Hee at Kumamoto Kuko Country Club.
