WBSC's Fraccari sees potential for Premier 12 to grow on global stage Riccardo Fraccari, the president of the World Baseball Softball Confederation, was in Sapporo for the Premier 12 opening game between Japan and South Korea on Nov. 8 before returning to Europe to attend an IOC summit in Lausanne. The Italian then traveled back to Japan for the final phase of his federation's flagship baseball championship in Tokyo on Wednesday night.
