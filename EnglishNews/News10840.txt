Taiwan's Huang ties course record Taiwan's Huang Yu-chen tied the course record with a 7-under-par 65 Saturday to take a two-shot lead into the final round of the NEC Karuizawa 72 tournament.
Huang moved 10-under 134 after a round featuring seven birdies at the Karuizawa 72 Golf North Course, while Yuri Fudo and U.S. LPGA player Akiko Fukushima climbed into a tie for second with Toshimi Kimura at 136 after 36 holes.
