New defense posture cause for mistrust: China Daily Japan's newly adopted defense guidelines are provocative and could threaten peace in the region while creating suspicion and distrust among the country's Asian neighbors, a Chinese state-run newspaper said in an editorial Monday.
Tokyo's adoption Friday of the "dynamic defense capability" concept, a shift from the prior basic concept designed to maintain the minimum necessary defense capability, could jeopardize regional peace and stability and hurt the country's postwar pacifist image, said the editorial, published in the English-language China Daily.
