Kim triumphs at NST Niigata Open South Korean tour veteran Kim Jong Duk took full advantage of his overnight lead to win the NST Niigata Open by five strokes Sunday for his fourth title on the Japanese tour.
Kim, who took a four-shot lead into the final round, had another steady round 67 at Forest Golf Club in Niigata Prefecture on his way to 21-under-par 263 and his first victory since the 1999 Yomiuri Open.
