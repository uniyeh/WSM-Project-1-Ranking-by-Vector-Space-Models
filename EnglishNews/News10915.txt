Sato leads by two Nobuhito Sato overcame back-to-back bogeys on the front nine for a 1-under-par 70 on Friday to take a two-stroke lead midway through the $140 million Fujisankei Classic golf tournament.
Sato carded three birdies from No. 9 over the Kawana Hotel's par-71 links course in Shizuoka Prefecture for a 5-under 137 and two-shot advantage over Takenori Hiraishi and overnight leader Lin Keng-chi of Taiwan.
